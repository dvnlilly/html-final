Read our story

Discover the world within your own backyard.

New York is the World wants to establish an accessible, living map and database of New York City (and its urbanized adjoining counties) that locates and assesses, every currently visible national/ethnic community and, every locality that currently maintains a significant identity.

We want users to be able to find the housing, markets, restaurants, events and institutions that represent every immigrant group in the shared spaces of the City.. Thank you.
